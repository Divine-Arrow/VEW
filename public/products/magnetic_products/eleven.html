MAGNETIC COOLANT SEPARATOR

Information :-

The Coolant Fluid with iron Dust particles Falling by Gravity to the Inlet of the Separator from the Precision Machine Tools such as Grinders, Milling Machines, Automates etc. The Coolant with Iron impurities come in to contact with Magnetic Drum and extracts all Iron particles

    It's Features are : -
  • Ideal for milling, grinding and general machining.
  • Inlet base in SS.
  • Remove particles from 5 micron upwards.
  • Complete with motor & double reduction gear box.
  • Operates with soluble or near cutting oils.
  • Efficient pre-filter for high levels of ferrous content.
  • Continuous automatic operation-maintenance free.
  • Rugged design for long-life.
  • Magnetic drum in High Power.
