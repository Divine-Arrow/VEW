High Pressure Hydraulic Machine Vice

Information :-

We are engaged in manufacturing and supplying of a varied assortment of High Pressure Hydraulic Machine Vice. These handy vices are manufactured using supreme quality raw materials and used in array of applications. Few of its attributes are durability, toughness, Unbreakable steel body, supreme performance and corrosion resistance. Made from latest equipments and modern design, these vices are available with lock down mechanism, clamping pressure 4000 kg (aprox), No hammering and no pipe locking.
Hardened & ground slide ways adds on to the reliability while using special tool materials.
Features Of High Pressure Hydraulic Machine Vice :-
• Unbreakable steel body, hardened & ground slide ways for longer repeated accuracy
• Accuracy in squareness with in 0.02mm
• No external Hydraulic Power Pack Required
• Trouble free Performance
• Extremely Easy to clamp & un-clamp.
• No hammering, no pipe locking.
• Supplied with or without swivel base
• clamping pressure 4000 kg(aprox)
• multi start screw rods are quick movement of hydraulic jaw & clamping
• lock down mechanism for minimum cant & lift of jaws.
