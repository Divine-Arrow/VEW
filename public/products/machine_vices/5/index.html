Self Centering Machine Vice

Information :-

The self-centering vice is very essential for doing precision jobs such as milling of accurate radial grooves and slots in round components as well as for drilling radial holes. The vices shown in this leaflet have two bases at right angles to each other which facilitate their being used both in horizontal and vertical positions. Both the bases are provided with T- slot tongues.
A single alignment on machine is enough for all work piece diameters. Once set, the central position remains constant even if the diameter of the job varies. The adjustable length stop facilitates the accurate determination of distances and the distance remains uncharged, which makes uninterrupted production.
